What's Inspected

Every Category in the 200-Point Inspection

A full Elevation Inspections walkthrough covers every visible and accessible system and component — here's a preview of what's on the checklist.

🏗️ Structure & Foundation

  • Foundation type and visible condition
  • Foundation walls — cracks, bowing, moisture
  • Floor framing and support posts
  • Grading and drainage away from foundation
  • Visible evidence of settling or movement
  • Sill plate condition

🌧️ Roof & Attic

  • Roof covering type, age, and condition
  • Flashing — chimney, valleys, penetrations
  • Gutters and downspouts
  • Attic access and framing
  • Attic insulation depth and type
  • Attic ventilation adequacy
  • Evidence of leaks or moisture damage

🏠 Exterior

  • Siding material and condition
  • Trim, caulking, and paint condition
  • Windows — operation, seals, glazing
  • Exterior doors — operation and weatherstripping
  • Deck, porch, and balcony structure
  • Driveway and walkway condition
  • Retaining walls

⚡ Electrical

  • Main electrical panel — type and condition
  • Breaker labeling and sizing
  • Visible wiring type and condition
  • GFCI protection in wet areas
  • Outlets — tested and grounded
  • Smoke and CO detector presence
  • Exterior lighting and fixtures

🚿 Plumbing

  • Water supply line material
  • Drain line material and condition
  • Water heater age, type, and condition
  • Water pressure and flow
  • Visible leaks at fixtures and connections
  • Shut-off valve locations
  • Hose bibs and exterior faucets

🌡️ Heating & Cooling

  • Furnace type, age, and condition
  • Heat exchanger — visible condition
  • Air conditioning — operation and condition
  • Ductwork — visible condition and sealing
  • Filter condition
  • Thermostat operation
  • Combustion air supply

🏠 Interior

  • Ceilings — stains, cracks, sag
  • Walls — damage, moisture, settlement cracks
  • Floors — condition and visible movement
  • Interior doors — operation and hardware
  • Windows — operation and locks
  • Stairways and handrails
  • Garage door safety reversal

🔥 Fireplace & Chimney

  • Firebox condition — cracks and spalling
  • Damper operation and seal
  • Smoke chamber condition
  • Chimney crown — cracking and sealing
  • Flashing at roof intersection
  • Chimney cap presence
  • Visible creosote buildup

🍳 Kitchen & Bathrooms

  • Cabinet condition and operation
  • Countertop condition
  • Sink, faucet, and drain function
  • Dishwasher operation
  • Range and oven operation
  • Exhaust fan function
  • Bathroom ventilation
  • Toilet — function and stability
