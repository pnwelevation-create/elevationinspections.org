What's Evaluated

Systematic Building Evaluation

🌧️

Exterior & Structure

Roof covering and drainage, foundation and grading, exterior walls, parking lot, walkways, and site drainage.

🏠

Interior

Flooring, walls, ceilings, stairways, exits, and visible evidence of moisture, past leaks, or mold.

⚡

Electrical

Panel condition, visible wiring, service capacity — observational assessment of the electrical system's current state.

🌡️

HVAC & Plumbing

Unit condition, visible ductwork, basic function. Fixtures, supply, drainage, and water heater inspection.
