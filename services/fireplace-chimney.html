Fireplace & Chimney Evaluation

A fireplace is only an asset if it's safe. Dimitriy checks the full system — firebox, flue, crown, flashing, and exterior structure.

Schedule Now — 509-305-3465
What Dimitriy Evaluates

A Complete Fireplace Safety Assessment

🔥

Firebox Condition

Cracks, spalling, and deteriorating mortar inside the firebox — all potential fire and smoke hazards that require attention.

🌀

Damper & Smoke Chamber

Damper operation and seal, smoke chamber condition — ensuring proper draft and preventing smoke from entering the home.

🏚️

Chimney Crown & Cap

Cracking in the chimney crown is the #1 entry point for water damage. Cap presence and condition keeps rain and animals out.

💧

Flashing

Proper seal between chimney and roof — failed flashing is a leading cause of water intrusion in Spokane homes.

🧱

Brick & Mortar

Exterior spalling, missing mortar, and structural leaning — issues that worsen rapidly through Spokane's freeze-thaw cycles.

⚠️

Safety & Clearances

Clearance from combustibles, visible creosote buildup (a fire hazard), and evidence of past repairs or unsafe alterations.

What You Receive

Written Report with Photos

A written report documenting the condition of the firebox, visible flue, and exterior chimney — with photos. Dimitriy will note any safety concerns and recommend follow-up with a certified chimney sweep if warranted.

The fireplace and chimney evaluation is included in the full home inspection, or can be scheduled as a standalone assessment.
