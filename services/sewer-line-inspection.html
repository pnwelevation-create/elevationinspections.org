Why It Matters

Your Standard Inspection Doesn't Cover the Sewer Line

The pipe running from your home to the city main — or to a septic system — goes completely unchecked in a standard home inspection. In Spokane and the surrounding area, older homes frequently have:

🌳

Root Intrusion

Mature trees crack and fill pipes over time — especially common in Spokane's established neighborhoods.

📉

Bellied Pipes

Sections that have settled and sag, causing waste to pool and creating chronic blockage issues.

💔

Cracked or Collapsed Lines

Especially common in homes built before the 1980s with clay or cast iron pipes reaching end of life.

🔩

Offset Joints

Pipe sections that have shifted apart — creating leaks, root entry points, and potential structural issues.

Sewer line replacement in Spokane typically costs $4,000–$15,000+ A sewer scope inspection is a fraction of that — and gives you negotiating power if problems are found.
