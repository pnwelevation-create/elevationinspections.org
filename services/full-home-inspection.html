What's Covered

Everything Visible & Accessible — Evaluated

A full home inspection with Elevation Inspections covers every visible and accessible component of the property, from the roof to the foundation. Here's what Dimitriy evaluates:

🏗️

Structure & Exterior

Foundation, grading, drainage, roof covering, gutters, flashing, exterior walls, siding, trim, windows, doors, driveway, walkways, deck, and porch.

🏠

Interior

Ceilings, walls, floors, visible defects and moisture signs. Stairways, railings, balconies. Attic insulation and ventilation. Basement and crawl space structure.

⚡

Electrical

Main panel, wiring condition, outlets, GFCI protection, grounding, visible hazards, and service entry.

🚿

Plumbing

Supply lines, drain lines, water heater condition and age, fixtures, visible leaks, pressure, and drainage function.

🌡️

HVAC

Heating and cooling systems, furnace/heat pump condition, ductwork, filters, thermostat operation, and visible deficiencies.

🍳

Built-In Appliances

Dishwasher, range, disposal, microwave (if built-in), and other installed appliances tested for basic function.
