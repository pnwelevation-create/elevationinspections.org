Who I Am

Born & Raised in Spokane. Built for This Work.

My name is Dimitriy. I was born and raised in the Spokane area — I've lived here for over 25 years — and I've spent 14 of those years working in residential and commercial property maintenance. I know this region's homes. I know how they're built, how they age, and where the problems tend to show up.

I started Elevation Inspections because I believe homebuyers deserve a real answer, not a rubber stamp.

When you hire me, you're not getting whoever's available that day. You get me, personally, at your property, walking every inch of it with the same attention I'd give if I were the one signing the purchase agreement.

That's not a tagline. That's the job.

My Standard

A Home Inspection Is Only Valuable If It's Honest

I will tell you what I find — clearly, completely, and without softening it to make a deal easier to close. You'll receive a full photo report the same day, written in plain language, covering everything I observed. If something concerns me, I'll tell you why and what it means in practical terms.

My goal isn't to kill deals. My goal is to make sure you never regret yours.
